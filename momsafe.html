Introduction

Empowering expectant mothers through AI-driven care.

Maternal health remains a critical global challenge, with disparities in access to quality care affecting millions of women.

As a UX Researcher & Designer working alongside two other UX researchers, I helped drive this project from research through final design — I conducted expert interviews with healthcare professionals, ran user research sessions with expectant mothers, and collaborated with my team to synthesize findings into personas and design principles. I designed the AI-powered maternal health companion in Figma and helped define the product's core features — daily health check-ins, trend analysis, and secure data options — grounded in the real pain points we uncovered through our research.

MomSAFE leverages large language models and machine learning to synthesize user inputs and clinical data, identify potential pregnancy risks, and deliver personalized, real-time pregnancy insights and proactive care recommendations — making evidence-based prenatal care more accessible.

Key Features

Built around what mothers actually need.

Every feature in MomSAFE was informed by research and designed to address specific gaps in the current maternal healthcare experience.

💬
AI Chatbot
24/7 multilingual support for maternal health inquiries — answering questions, providing guidance, and connecting mothers to resources any time of day.
📊
Survey Check-ins
Regular symptom tracking for mood, physical health, and pregnancy milestones — building a longitudinal view of each mother's journey.
📅
Appointment Booking
Direct access to healthcare providers with streamlined scheduling — reducing the friction between recognizing a concern and getting professional care.
🔍
Data-Driven Insights
AI-powered trend analysis that surfaces patterns across check-ins — proactively flagging potential risks before they become emergencies.
🔐
Privacy & Security
User-controlled data with transparent policies — because trust is non-negotiable when it comes to sensitive health information. Every mother decides what to share and with whom.

Final Prototype

Designed for accessibility, inclusivity, and trust.

The final design prioritizes clarity and calm. A minimalist UI keeps the experience clutter-free and easy to navigate, with a clear content structure that guides mothers through their health data without overwhelming them.

Predictive AI-driven insights are surfaced contextually — so mothers see what matters most at the right moment, helping prevent maternal health risks before they escalate.

🎯
Accessibility
Simplified language, multilingual support, and intuitive navigation for mothers of all backgrounds and tech comfort levels.
🤲

🛡️
