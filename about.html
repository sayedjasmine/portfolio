I'm Jasmine Sayed — a Product Designer and UX Researcher pursuing my Master's in Human Centered Design & Engineering at the University of Washington ('27).

My design journey started with my Girl Scout Gold Award, where I built my first website and realized tech could actually make a difference for people.

Since then I've worked with Microsoft, Amazon, and a handful of startups building research-driven, human-centered experiences — especially at the crossroads of AI and accessibility.
