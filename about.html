About

A little more about me.

I'm a product designer and UX researcher, most recently a UX designer on Microsoft's CoreAI team, where one usability study turned a deprioritized Severity-4 bug that hit 8 of 8 participants into a priority fix. I hold a Master's in Human Centered Design and Engineering from the University of Washington.

My work spans identity, accessibility, climate tech, and maternal health, with AI, accessibility, and trust as the throughlines. Published in the TAGA Journal and a participant in CHI's Student Design Competition.

When I'm not in Figma, I'm teaching preschool or organizing hackathons. Both have taught me as much about design as any class has.
